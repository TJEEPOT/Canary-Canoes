Clubhouse

Relax and Train

-Improved for 2017!-

We have recently renovated our Clubhouse to accomodate the growing needs of our club! We now have more toilets and seating, new vending machines and we've overhauled the reception area too.

Meet with your friends before heading out onto the water with your instructor.

With boat storage on site, you will never be more than 10 minutes from getting your canoe on the water here on the riverside. If you don't currently own a canoe or kayak, you can hire one from our club on an hourly basis, ask us for full details.

Then when you're finished on the water, come back to the warm clubhouse and enjoy a cooldown in our showers and enjoy a snack in the main hall.

For those days when you're not looking to get on the water, we have a fully stocked land-based training gym with paddling and rowing machines, as well as weights training, crosstrainers and treadmills in our fully-stocked facility.

In addition to canoe storage, we also have access to trailers to help members get across the country to partisipate in our trips and events.
