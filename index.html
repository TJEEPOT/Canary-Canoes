Welcome!

Canary Canoes is a kayak and canoe club based in the idyllic grounds of Earlham Park in Norwich, Norfolk.

We welcome new members of any age and we have a range of skillsets within the club, ranging from beginners to national champions.

We hope that you enjoy what we have here, and please feel free to contact us if you are interested in joining.

See you on the water!
